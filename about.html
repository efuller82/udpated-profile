A bit about me

I am a father of three, a husband of one, and a former bartender/event manager of 16 years. For some time, I've had the dream of learning to be a web developer. I made a plan and a few months ago, I quit my full-time job and began to set that plan in motion, joining the Coding Bootcamp at UT Austin.

So far on this journey I have learned a lot, and one of the most important things I've learned is how much more there is to be learned. My passion for web development has increased with each rewarding day and I look forward to a long and rewarding career.
